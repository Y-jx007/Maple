弯曲时空的狄拉克方程

2023-05-22 18:58:19 · 3 min · 634 words

Lorentz联络

采用几何单位制，令$\hbar,c,G=1$，

设基$h_\mu$下度规$g_{\mu\nu}$在基$e_\mu=\Lambda_{\mu}^{\;\nu} h_\nu$下化为$\eta_{\mu\nu}=\text{diag}(+,-,-,-)$，$\Lambda_{\mu}^{\;\nu}$为洛伦兹变换矩阵，$\eta_{\mu\nu}=\Lambda_{\mu}^{\;\rho}\Lambda_{\nu}^{\;\sigma}g_{\rho\sigma}$，两组基下狄拉克矩阵

$$\lbrace \gamma^\mu,\gamma^\nu\rbrace=2\eta^{\mu\nu}\qquad \lbrace \overline{\gamma}^\mu,\overline{\gamma}^\nu\rbrace=2g^{\mu\nu}$$

旋量变换$\tilde{\psi}_e=L\psi_h$，协变导数$D_\mu \psi=\partial_\mu\psi+\Gamma_\mu \psi$，要求$\tilde{D}_\mu\tilde{\psi}=L D_\mu \psi$，可得

$$\tilde{\Gamma}_\mu=L\Gamma_\mu L^{-1}-\partial_\mu L^{-1}$$

对矩阵$M$有

$$D_\mu M=\nabla_\mu M+[\Gamma_\mu,M]$$

再要求$D_\mu$与度规适配，$D_\mu g^{\nu\rho}=0$，由于$\lbrace \overline{\gamma}^\mu,\overline{\gamma}^\nu\rbrace=2g^{\mu\nu}$，选择充分条件$D_\mu \overline{\gamma}^\nu=0$

$$\partial_\mu \overline{\gamma}^\nu+\Gamma_{\mu\rho}^\nu\overline{\gamma}^\rho+\Gamma_\mu\overline{\gamma}^\nu-\overline{\gamma}^\nu\Gamma_\mu=0$$

纯空间转动

狄拉克方程一般形式$i\hbar\gamma^\mu D_\mu\psi=i\hbar\gamma^\mu(e_\mu+\Gamma_\mu)\psi=mc\psi$

转动柱坐标系下线元

$$\begin{aligned}
\dd s^2&=c^2\dd t^2-\dd r^2-r^2(\dd \phi+\omega\dd t)^2-\dd z^2\\
\qquad\\
&=\left(1-\frac{\omega^2r^2}{c^2}\right)\left(c\dd t-\frac{1}{c}\frac{\omega r^2}{\displaystyle\left(1-\frac{\omega^2r^2}{c^2}\right)}\dd\phi\right)^2-\dd r^2-\frac{ r^2}{\displaystyle\left(1-\frac{\omega^2r^2}{c^2}\right)}\dd \phi^2-\dd z^2\\
\qquad\\
&\overset{\omega r/c\ll 1}{\approx} \left(c\dd t-\frac{\omega r^2}{c}\dd \phi\right)^2-\dd r^2-(r\dd\phi)^2-\dd z^2\\
\qquad\\
&:=(\theta^0)^2-(\theta^1)^2-(\theta^2)^2-(\theta^3)^2
\end{aligned}$$

由 $e_\mu \theta^\nu=\delta_\mu^\nu$ 可得基矢量

$$e_0=\frac{1}{c}\partial_t\qquad e_1=\partial_r\qquad e_2=\frac{1}{r}\partial_\phi+\frac{\omega r}{c^2}\partial_t\qquad e_4=\partial_z$$

对易子$[e_\mu,e_\nu]=c_{\mu\nu}^\lambda e_\lambda$，非零系数只有

$$\displaystyle c_{21}^2=-c_{12}^2=\frac{1}{r}\qquad c_{12}^0=-c_{21}^0=\frac{2\omega}{c}$$
